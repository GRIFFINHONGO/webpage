GAME DAY 5
Kabarak University headed to Nairobi to face the log leader Nondiescript Rfc at the Ngong race course in what would turn out to be a one sided match with the varsity side being shown no mercy. The game was a very physical game but that did not scare the men in red and white stripes since that is just how rugby is in the end. However besides being quite a physicalgame with five players from Kabarak getting injuries the varsity side could not keep up in the end and ended up suffering a heavy defeat of 46:6
