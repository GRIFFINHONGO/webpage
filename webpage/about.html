Kabarak University rugby club is a team founded in the Rift valley region in the year 2018.
The team started off in the nationwide league under coach Lawrence Buyachi who would soon depart and go ahead to play for Kabras & Kenya Simbas
Mr.Leonard Ngetich would then take over and see the team through to the playoffs where they emerged victorious and got a promotion to the CHAMPIONSHIP LEAGUE where they currently play.
The team has training sessions four times a week and encourages interested players to join as they seek KENYA CUP promotion
