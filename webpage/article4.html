GAME DAY 10
Kabarak University welcome University of Eldoret to Nakuru a week after sufferinga huge lose in Kisumu to Kisumu Rfc knocking them out off the play offs ending their run for a promotion to the Kenya Cup league. The game started slow with U.O.E taking a 7:0 lead which wasn't long lived as Kabarak University would bounce back making taking a lead with 1st half scores at 14:10. The 2nd half would then turn out to be one sided with U.O.E on the receiving end of the attacks by Kabarak University. The visitors would then suffer an upset and ended up refusing to play and as a result the game was discontinued shortly into the start of the 2nd half with scores at 29:10 and the boys in red and white stripes would take the win setting a record of never suffering a defeat in their home grounds throughout the season.
